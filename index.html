Q: What is the education system like in Turkey?

A: The education system in Turkey is structured into primary, secondary, and higher education levels. Primary education is compulsory, and higher education institutions offer various programs.

Q: How many universities are there in Turkey?

A: Turkey has numerous universities, with both public and private institutions. The total number is subject to change as new universities may be established.

Q: Can international students study in Turkey?

A: Yes, Turkey welcomes international students. Many universities offer programs in English, and there are various scholarships available for international students.
